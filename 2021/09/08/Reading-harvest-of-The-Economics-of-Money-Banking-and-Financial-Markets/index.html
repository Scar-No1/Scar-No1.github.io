Reading harvest of The Economics of Money,Banking, and Financial Markets

Posted on 2021-09-08

“The Economics of Money,Banking, and Financial Markets” is written by Frederic S. Mishkin. It has been used as a teaching material. This book illustrates the basic concepts and theories in finance.

From this book, I found a lot of connections between the economics I studied in school and the financial theories in the books. For example, Fisher effect proves the relationship between expected inflation rate and interest rate. the predict inflation rate increases will lead to the demand for bond to decrease as the expected return on bonds relative to real assets has fallen. According to the theory I learned at school,Therefore, the interest rate will increase as the the price of the bond and interest rate is negative corelated.

Also, it analyses how credit rating agencies and subprime mortgage bonds such as CDO caused the financial bubble and the financial crisis in 2008, which provide great evidence and theoretical foundations.

The understanding of the asset management industry

Posted on 2021-09-08

When I read the book “ monetary finance”, I was confused about the concept of “ sort of bank credit assets “, I discussed this topic with my mother, and consulted with related experience of the elder. Hence, i got involved in and initially discussed a relatively unpopular topic in the financial industry, namely “distressed asset Management”, or “AMC”. What I learned later aroused my interest: AMC industry started late in China, with huge scale and broad market prospect, but there are very few professionals engaged in this industry. After an internship at an asset management company, I got a better understanding of the industry.

Generally speaking, the NPL in the market refer to the NPL of financial institutions and more to the non-performing creditor’s rights of banks, among which the most important is the non-performing loan (refers to the loan that the bank customers cannot repay the principal and interest on schedule and in amount. ).

For the banks, non-performing assets are a burden and drag, non-performing assets shackle hands and feet, so that the bank is difficult to implement development; For investors in bad assets, the huge profit space of bad assets is very attractive, so that countless investors participate in it one after another. From the perspective of investors, the current global economic downturn, downward pressure is huge, the development of many areas are facing bottlenecks, even challenges, and non-performing assets, known as the best counter-cyclical investment, is thriving and booming. From the perspective of financial institutions’ risk prevention and sound operation, it is very important and necessary to divest and dispose of non-performing assets, just like a sick person has to seek medical treatment or even surgical resection.

Bad assets are not worthless assets. The famous investment guru Graham once said, “There are no bad assets to invest in, only bad prices to buy.” What’s more, most of the time these assets are not worthless junk because of a lack of proper operations or temporary difficulties due to the economic cycle. Therefore, smart NPL investors will practice contrarian thinking, buy at the right price, dispose of it properly, and then wait for the right time to sell it, thus making a huge profit. In fact, NPL is a typical industry of turning waste into treasure. For excellent gold diggers, NPL disposal industry hides huge treasure. Westerners refer to distressed asset management companies (AMCs) as “vultures that eat rotten meat”.

I also learned that China’s finance started very late, and there is still a big gap between China and Europe and America. From a global perspective, the best choice to study accounting and finance is to go to the UK. Britain’s financial industry is highly developed. According to OECD statistics, the UK’s GDP in 2019 was 2,214.8 billion pounds, of which the value of finance and insurance was 129.6 billion pounds, accounting for more than 5%. Currently, the UK is the world’s largest exporter of financial services, is the global influence of the international financial center of London, London ranked first in the total cross-border banking, London has the world’s largest foreign exchange market, London is in the world in the field of international bond issuance and trading, gold, silver trading volume in the world’s first.

Therefore, I really hope to study accounting and finance in university. This will help me better enter the asset management industry in the future.

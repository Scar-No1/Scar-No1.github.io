Reading harvest of Currency war

This series of book was written by a Chinese scholars in 2006. It discusses who was behind many of the most important events in history and how they used financial instruments to achieve their goal. There are five books in this series. I have finished first two.

I was impressed by the special financial analysis perspective in the book which I have never learned before. The book is mainly focusing on “Currency “. It points out that currency is the right to claim wealth and what kind of wealth is used as collateral to issue money determines the nature of money. In the past, the collateral was gold. In the financial history, a group of people were passionate to change this situation, which caused the currency war around the gold standard and silver standard in order to control the right to issue currency.

I was attract by the the allure of currency. From reading this book, I deeply understand that finance is the subject that study how to create value by using the concepts of time and trust. It is both astonishingly creative and constructive, but also destructive.

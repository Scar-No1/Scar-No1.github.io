Day 1

I learned how to check the bill. I contact with the proof of charge to an account.

Here is the process

  1. Get the invoice for the transaction
  2. determine whether it purchase orders or sales orders
  3. Read the important information (cargo,name,quantity,price,payment for goods,tax rate,tax)
  4. Check the payment for goods and tax by adding up them on each invoice and comparing with the value in the system.
  5. Record the value to the companyâ€™s system on computer
  6. Print to get the proof of charge to an account

Theory

Tax payable = output tax(payed when selling) - input tax(payed when selling)

The invoice limit of different company is different, this limit decides by tax bureau

A purchase invoice is an invoice issued by the seller to the purchaser, vice versa.

Notes I have made

Day 2

  • Checking account can prevent the objective data errors in the transaction process and can also prevent accounting fraud.
  • Online bank payment
    • A transaction needs to be reviewed by the department head, the auditor, the finance minister, and the payer before it can be paid.
    • The items that need to be reviewed include the collection unit, the payment unit, the department, the amount and the remittance method.
  • The main difference between electronic bank draft and electronic commercial bank draft is that the acceptor is a bank rather than an enterprise

Day 3

  • Invoicing requires special invoicing software that is not internal to the company.
  • The invoices used are special invoices for value-added tax.
  • When making out an invoice, pay attention to the number of the invoice and the number of the computer system
  • The invoice has three couplets:
    • the first one is black for the internal accounting of the financial department
    • the second one is green for the accounting of the purchasing staff
    • the third one is yellow for the tax declaration of the purchasing staff

The title and abstract of the essay

Study on the Sustainable Factors of Fair activities

Liu Muyang, Han Jiarui

(International Department of Nanjing Jinling High School, Nanjing, Jiangsu, 210005)

Abstract: as a traditional sales mode, fair activity is not only a commercial trade mode, but also a carrier of traditional trade culture. This paper constructs a conceptual model of market sustainable development based on life cycle theory, and puts forward six factors: resource capacity, operation capacity, policy environment, economic environment, social environment and technological environment. Based on the reliability and validity analysis of the sample data, this paper uses Likert five scale to conduct a questionnaire survey, and verifies that resource capacity and policy environment factors significantly support the fair sustainability.

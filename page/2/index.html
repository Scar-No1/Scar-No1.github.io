  • Four characteristics of business model:

​ - Effectiveness

​ - Endogeneity

​ - Creativity

​ - Sustainability

  • Emphasize the importance of building the internal core competitiveness and continuously gaining profits

  • The business model is not static
    Corporate social responsibility + triple bottom line — corporate value proposition

  • determines sustainability — economic proposition, social proposition and ecological proposition

  • The essence of sustainable business model: enterprises pursue economic sustainability (basic), social sustainability and environmental sustainability through a series of business activities

  • Business model sustainability is realized under the joint action of external environment and internal factors

  • Business sustainable development: its elements form a harmonious relationship (internal consistency, external rationality)

  • Coordination relationship breaking — innovation activities — technology, organizational structure (Management), social and Ecological Innovation

  • Achieving economic goals is the premise! (profit) – > create excellent financial management system

  • A complete ecological environment

  • Policy and government support (e.g. subsidies, related infrastructure) are also guarantees of sustainability

  • Consumers are more willing to choose products and services under the concept of sustainability

  • Sustainable development theory

    • Theory: not only to meet the development needs of contemporary people, but also to avoid threatening the development ability of future generations

    • Summarize the theory

      • Maintain the vitality of enterprises
        Meet business objectives
        Continuous profit growth
      • achieve harmonious coexistence with the external environment
        -At the same time, we need to pay attention to the needs of environment, ecology, society and culture

  • Life cycle theory

    • Connotation:

      • Consider the research object as an organism

      • The stage of birth, growth, maturity and decline

      • The object of study can be replicated and changed

      • How the research object exchanges with the outside world, and what are the characteristics or commonalities in the process of phase transformation

      • The death of life is not determined by the external environment, but by the internal structure of the organization

    • Life cycle model:

      Round, rectangular, spiral, wavy
      Analyse the cyclical characteristics of market business model and select the corresponding model

  • Enterprise capability theory

    • Enterprise resource theory

      • the heterogeneity of enterprise resources leads to the gap of enterprise competitiveness

      • Tangible resources, intangible resources (highest value), human resources (p16)

      • Better resources — better profitability

      • The effectiveness of material resources is based on the use of personnel

    • The theory of enterprise core competence

      • Definition of enterprise core competence: knowledge collection within the enterprise, especially those knowledge that can effectively combine different production technologies and different technologies

      • Features: extensibility, user value, uniqueness

      • Core competence: tap potential market opportunities, meet customer needs, difficult to imitate

    • Theory of enterprise dynamic capability

      • Definition of dynamic capability: in order to achieve rapid adaptation and good survival in the changing external environment, an organization can effectively integrate, reconstruct and mobilize its internal capabilities

      • Improvisation: the ability to meet challenges purposefully

      • Intention, character, clarity

Bitcoin

In today’s Reading Senimer, I watched a video about Bitcoin, which I found it very interesting.

What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a cryptocurrency invented in 2008 by an unknown person called Satoshi Nakamoto and started in 2009 when its implementation was released as open-source software. It is a decentralized digital currency without a central bank or single administrator that can be sent from user to user on the peer-to-peer bitcoin network without the need for intermediaries. Transactions are verified by network nodes through cryptography and recorded in a public distributed ledger called a blockchain.

How to obtain Bitcoins?

There are two main method to obtain Bitcoin.

First is using common currency to exchange bitcoin. The exchange rate now is 1 bitcoin for 18980.8 US Dollar.

The second method is mining. Mining is a way which using computer to process a programme, when the result processing out is with in a range, the processer which also called miner can get a reward of bitcoins.

Mining

It is a record-keeping service done through the use of computer processing power. Miners keep block chain consistent, and unalterable by repeatedly grouping newly broadcast transactions into a block, which is then broadcast to the network and verified by recipient nodes. Each block contains a SHA-256 of the previous block, thus linking it to the previous block and giving the blockchain its name.

Early bitcoin miners used GPUs for mining, as they were better suited to the proof-of -work algorithm than CPUs. Later amateurs minded bitcoins with specialized FPGA and ASIC chips. Today, high-performance mining hardware is used.

Paragraph writing:the biggest failure I experienced

This series of blogs are all stort paragraphs which record my daily experiences, feelings and thoughts. These can be used for the essay writing.

The biggest failure I experienced

Failure has happened to everyone, and sometimes they are caused by success.

The most painful experience I’ve ever had was at the end of my eighth grade when I came first in my final exam. I still clearly remember how ecstatic I was when I got the report card. I couldn’t wait to tell my classmates the good news and enjoy their admiration. I felt so proud of my achievement and felt like everything would come easily to me in the future. There was no doubt that I was overconfident.

Later that day, I was informed by my teacher to share the successful experiences at the parents’ meeting the following week. That was a surprising news for me, I had visions of professional speakers giving wonderful talks to enthralled audiences and I felt that was who I was; an experienced performer going to the stage calmly, having loud voice, clear logic and giving some creative ideas from time to time. So, in the following days before the parents’ meeting, I didn’t do any preparation. I kept telling myself that if I had got an A grade, then a speech would be simple.

But of course I would pay a heavy price for this arrogance and blind self- confidence and it became immediately apparent on stage that there was a gap between my ideals and reality. I finally realized a gap did exist between ideal and reality when standing on the stage. Suddenly, I had a blank mind and words failed me. Without any preparation, I couldn’t read the speech text directly so as to avoid embarrassment, I had to use some trite principles to fill the gaps in my mind. The speech was completely illogical and boring, with several seconds of silence when I couldn’t say a word. I felt awful. Seeing some parents sitting beside the stage becoming a little impatient, my self-confidence disappeared. My voice became smaller and smaller. I finished my speech in a hurry and ran out of the classroom without looking up. I felt ashamed and considered this as my biggest failure.

Success can be used as a driving force for people to move forward or as a reason for laziness. The lesson I have learnt from this humbling experience is that confidence is essential but overconfidence can lead to problems. As the saying goes, failure is the mother of success. Both failure and success should be faced accordingly on their own terms.

The ways to expand the business of a restaurant

Han

Nowadays, people concern more about what they eat and how the eat, which promote the rapid development of catering industry. It is surprising found that the growth rate of this industry is more than 10% higher than that of other industries in recent years. [Need a reference for this statement] Many small restaurants have to expand their business in order to maintain competitiveness in the market. [why?] For the topic of how a popular local restaurant could expand its business and operate successfully in different regions, I suggest that both chain and franchise model can be considered.

In terms of the chain model, it is an operation method which manage many stores in different regions of country or world with the same brand, same products and same store policies by the same parent company.[Again, need a reference] The parent company has full control of his chain stores, and as a result, it needs to take responsibility for everything of his branches, including profits. In China, there is a famous hotpot restaurant, which is operated in the form of chain store, called Haidilao Hot Pot. So far, it has already had 117 chain stores all over China. Through 20 years of hard work, Haidilao gradually has more than 20000 employees from an unknown hot pot brand. [You could have used the Haidilao corporate report as a reference]

Chain model has many advantages. First, because of the complete control of the owning chain stores, it can perfectly present its successful business philosophy, such as the ways of cooking and the sources of food material, avoiding many unnecessary costs while expanding. Second, the owner can receive high revenue from every chain store [that they own] of his. Apart from the basic cost for operating the shops, the brand owner can receive all rest of the money. However, disadvantages exist. There is a huge amount of initial cost when new branches are built. This is because the parent company should take responsibility to everything of the branches, including the cost of construction and decoration. Also, opening chain stores has a huge demand for management talents, which it’s difficult to hire. If any branch goes wrong in management, the company and its owner will bear a great loss, both in financial and reputation aspect. [A good discussion, but needs to be supported by references]

Another way to expand is running the franchising model. A franchise business is a business owned by an entrepreneur, offering products or services labeled by a corporation that provide assistance in every aspect of business, in return for fees based on profits. [Again, need a reference]To expand the business scale, the brand owner need to hire employees. But the owner won’t make rules on how the employees run their franchising store. [Most franchises (such as Subway) have strict rules in the contract on how the franchise is run. The franchisee will have control over staffing] He just negotiate with them about the profit sharing and make a contract. In this way, the business can expand into a very large scale in a very short time, one example is that “A Little”, a popular milk-tea shop in China, it has successfully expand over 2000 stores in China which only spent about 15 years. [Again, need a reference]

For the advantages of this operation method, franchising can expand quickly and has little initial cost [to the franchise owner], which is especially suitable for those small catering business. The owner don’t need to pay anything for the opening of one of his franchising store because the whole managing tasks as well as responsibility are undertook by his employees. What’s more, the brand owner won’t bear too much risk to go bankrupt. Since there is no much initial cost and investment, though some of the stores may lose money in business, this has little effect on the total profit revenue. However, one serious drawback of franchising model is that the reputation of the brand image may be easily damaged. Without any guidance, the employees may make fatal mistakes which lead to bad reputation to the whole brand. [Also it may cost a lot of time and money to prepare the franchise rules and build the brand]

In a word, both method to expand the local restaurant’s business, chain model and franchising model, have advantages and drawbacks. Chain model can help the owner control the chain stores well and receive high revenue from each shop, but it has high initial cost and poor management will cause great loss. Franchising model expand quickly and has little initial cost and bear relatively low risk, but the reputation of brand may be damaged easily. For the local popular restaurant, the owner needs to determine which model to choose according to his own financial and workers’ condition, or combine two method together in order to expand its business and operate successfully in different regions.

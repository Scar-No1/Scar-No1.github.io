Survey on the sustainability factors of the fair development

Posted on 2020-12-27 | In Economics and Statistics

This programme began about half a month ago. I made a team with one of my schoolmate. The main purpose of this programme is to study the sustainability factors of fair is today’s situation and we need to finish an essay in the end.

Stage

  1. Preview
  2. Market research
  3. analysis the data
  4. finish the essay

Preview

We spent two weeks on this period. In this stage, we did some research about the examples of some world famous fair.

  • Anpawa market, Bangkok, Thailand
    • short time
  • Indian moonlight Market (started from 1650)
  • Camden market, UK
    • Rock punk style
  • creative fair
    • Nautilus Market(鹦鹉螺市集)
    • “Simple” fair
    • Shenyang Lu Xun Academy of Fine Arts—- Star fair
  • Brick Lane Market
    • It is a London market centered on brick land, located in Tower Hamlets in East London. It’s at the north end of Brick Lane, in the heart of East London’s Bengal community.
    • Theme: Art, design, second hand, snack, culture
  • Portobello market
    • Theme: antique

We mainly analysis these examples from the following aspect:

  • Time range
  • Theme
  • Size
  • Organizational form
  • Profit
  • sustainability factor(questionnaire)
  • the influence to the city development

Here are the several factors that we analysed in the first place:

  • profitability
  • environment (PEST)
  • commodity uniqueness
  • reputation of the stall owner or market
  • product and market model innovation
  • enterprise capacity
  • holding time (Festival)

These are quite general factors, which we can establish index system according to these reasons.

e.g. 沈阳鲁迅美术学院 星星市集 一年两场 （全国性）

市集的可持续性研究

市集活动 临时性 无固定组织管理 非盈利性

目的： 提出市集活动的可持续发展的策略

研究内容：

  1. 背景： 定义； 发展历史； 分类定位（主题）与特点（网上，实地调研）

  2. 世界各地持续/不持续举办市集活动的案例分析：时间跨度；主题特色；规模（铺位数); 管理组织形式； 盈利模式； 可持续性/不可持续性的原因 （调查问卷）； 市集活动对于城市发展的推动作用

  3. 市集活动可持续性： 发展因素分析； 指标体系构建； 调查问卷编制

    4.问卷调查，数据收集分析

    5.市集活动的可持续发展策略

案例中市集可持续性的原因（一级指标）：盈利，环境（PEST），商品唯一性，摊主或市集的口碑，产品与市集模式创新，企业能力, 举办时间（节庆）

  • PEST: 环境分析方法 P: 政治 E：经济 S：社会 T：技术

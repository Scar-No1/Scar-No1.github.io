C11-project-Brand-Feiyue

Background

In such a market which full of competition, traditional retail model gradually shows its drawbacks, inflexible, low efficiency and the mismatch between online and offline. The era of 100% online marketing is over. The new retail model, which the focus of work gradually shift from online to offline, booms up.

Target brand

  • Feiyue

Brand introduction

History

- set up in 1958, produced liberation shoes

- in 1963, began to produce track shoes

- in 1997, brand Feiyue was registered

- in 2005, a French Patrice BAstian promote the brand Feiyue abroad,the shoes were loved by foreigners.

- after 2005, Feiyue began to promote in China again, considered as national fashion

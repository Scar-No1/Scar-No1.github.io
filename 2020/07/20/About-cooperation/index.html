About cooperation

It is necessary for me to record some of the problems that I confronted in life in order to have self- examination.

I have attended a business programe called 8XP, teaching me about practical business knowledge and helping make a store design. I was distributed in Group A, which is consisted with all senior three students, except me and it seems that they all know each other since the are classmates. I gradually found that it is hard for me to integrate into their discussion. They usually have more immediate thinking and know more about brands than me, so I can seldom come up with any novel point of view.

Confidence is the thing that I need most. I think it is quite important for me to be brave to express my opinion. I Should not be afraid of making mistakes since it is the process of learning.

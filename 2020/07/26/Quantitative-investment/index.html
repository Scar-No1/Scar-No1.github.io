Quantitative investment

Posted on 2020-07-26 | In Business study

This is the first time I heard this word, “quantitative investment”.

It can be considered as a investment which totally bases on data.(From Google: Quantitative investing, also known as systematic investing, is an investment approach that uses advanced mathematical modelling, computer systems and data analysis to calculate the optimal probability of executing a profitable trade.)

Stock certification

Stock is all of the shares into which ownership of the corporation is divided. It can be trade on the stock market. Imagine that you want to buy an apple, the original price of one apple is 3 yuan. A lot of people want to buy it, so some people are willing to buy it in 3.1 yuan. Hence, the price of an apple now becomes 3.1 yuan. As the price of apple get higher, less people want to buy it in the price of 3.1 yuan, so the price of an an apple then drops back to 3 yuan. You can consider the apple as a share. That is the reason why the stock value fluctuate all the time.

How is the quantitative investment carried out

First, the computer analysis the data of stock market in previous year and it can predict whether the stock value will raise or drop. Then, computer can judge if the investors need to buy this stock or sell through a programme.

Characteristics of quantitative investment

  1. It is carried out by a computer. In reality, the data needed is over 100T , which people can only rely on computer to circulate.

  2. People believe the result from the computer in most conditions, unless the result goes against the financial principles.

  3. A lot of math knowledge are used when programming, e.g. linear regression(which also be used in AI)

  4. What a programme needs to improve most is its operating speed since other people can buy stocks when the price is low and sell it at higher price but you can only buy the stock which other people sell(higher price) and sell at low price.

It is hard for people to speculate in stock in the future because more and more people use computer to finish the task which is much more efficient than those people who do market research.

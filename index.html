The title and abstract of the essay

Study on the Sustainable Factors of Fair activities

Liu Muyang, Han Jiarui

(International Department of Nanjing Jinling High School, Nanjing, Jiangsu, 210005)

Abstract: as a traditional sales mode, fair activity is not only a commercial trade mode, but also a carrier of traditional trade culture. This paper constructs a conceptual model of market sustainable development based on life cycle theory, and puts forward six factors: resource capacity, operation capacity, policy environment, economic environment, social environment and technological environment. Based on the reliability and validity analysis of the sample data, this paper uses Likert five scale to conduct a questionnaire survey, and verifies that resource capacity and policy environment factors significantly support the fair sustainability.

Now we start learning how to do the reliability and validity analysis.

信度分析：

信度分析：信度指测验结果的一致性，稳定行和可靠性

采取同样的方法进行重复测量，其所得结果相一致的程度

Cronbach’s a系数法： < 0.6 信度不足 0到1 越大越好

效度分析： 检测测量工具能够准确测出需测事物的程度

（Amos分析）

如差异小于阈值，则认为假设模型与实际数据你和程度良好

（验证因子分析）

结构方程建模 结构效度（绝对拟合度 增值拟合度 综合拟合度） 收敛效度（CR组合信度 AVE平均方差提取量) >0.7好

区分效度（变量之间相关性）

Reliability analysis

reliability analysis: reliability refers to the consistency of test results, stable line and reliability. The same method is used for repeated measurement, and the results are consistent
Cronbach’s α coefficient method: < 0.6, the greater the reliability is, the better

Validity analysis: the measurement tools can accurately measure the degree of things to be tested (Amos analysis)
If the difference is less than the threshold, it is considered that the hypothetical model is in good agreement with the actual data (confirmatory factor analysis)

Structural validity of structural equation modelling (absolute fit, incremental fit, comprehensive fit) convergence validity (CR combination reliability, AVE mean variance extraction) > 0.7 good

discriminant validity (correlation between variables)

The Tool Used

  1. SPSS Statistic
  2. Amos

Data set

Reliability analysis

Using the tool SPSS, we calculate the Cronbach’s α coefficient. For this test index, if the result is not less than 0.9 , then it means the data collected has great internal consistency, reliability and stability.

Validity analysis

We use Amos to build the model and calculate several index, namely χ2 /df , IFI, TLI, CFI, RSEMA.

Also, the model can calculate the composite reliability(CR), the average variance extracted(AVE) and the discriminant validity.

However, the result of the first analysis is not so good. Several index and the discriminant validity fail to meet good requirements. Hence, we rescreened the data and delete some uneffective data. We now have 383 valid questionnaires.

Paragraph writing Who I am

Writing this paragraph can help me correctly understand myself and prepare for the future professional choice.

Topic

Tell us about who you are. How would your family, friends, and/or members of your community describe you? If possible, please include something about yourself that you are most proud of and why. (maximum 350 words)

My paragraph

I[YL1] am Han, a 16 years old international school student.

My parents describe me as a boy who has recently grown up, both physically and psychologically. I am now much taller[YL2] and stronger, more independent and mature[YL3] , more hardworking and organized than I was previously.

In my friends’ eyes[YL4] , I am a humorous and helpful person who likes[YL5] laughing a lot. Moreover, they think[YL6] I have a strong sense of teamwork[YL7] , which is the quality that I feel most proud of[YL8] . This is a very valuable quality since people have to work in teams throughout[YL9] their lives. Strong teamwork can help a group work efficiently though I may sometimes need to make some sacrifices[YL10] . For example, I often have[YL11] to do more work than any other teammate. Though it seems unfair, I am still[YL12] willing to take on[YL13] this task for the sake of[YL14] achieving the[YL15] team’s goal. Also, I get a sense of personal satisfaction from working well as part of a team[YL16] [YL17] [YL18] .

[YL1]Use paragraph indents and line spacing to make your writing easier to read

[YL2]If you use the comparative – “er” or” more than” you have to compare the adjectives to something else. For example, “She is smarter than her sister.” Or you just say “She is smart”. So here you need to compare yourself to how you were before.

[YL3]We use the adjective “mature” to describe a person.

[YL4]😊 “In my friends’ eyes” is a great idiom to use.

[YL5]Who is third person which takes an “s”

[YL6]It is interesting to reflect. Do you think that because your friends perceive you humorous, helpful and a teamworker, this encourages you to display those characteristics? According to sociologist Charles Horton Cooley a person’s behaviour and self-esteem are dictated by how they are seen by others. In this way, perception is reality. An interesting concept when you consider who you are.

[YL7]The word we use is “teamwork”

[YL8]The phrase is to be most proud of

[YL9]The appropriate word here is “throughout” which means for the duration of

[YL10]What sacrifices do you have to make? It would be good to develop this point. Your essay is rather short, so it would be interesting to expand.

[YL11]You need to add “often” to clarify what you are saying

[YL12]“still” not “stills”

[YL13]The phrasal verb to “take on” is good here, It means to undertake a task or responsibility, especially a difficult one. For example: I don’t feel ready to take on new responsibilities.

[YL14]“for the sake of” is a good expression to use here. It means for the purpose or motive of

[YL15]Singular countable nouns always have an article – a/an or the (or another determiner – my, your , this, that etc.

[YL16]This is rather unclear. I think you need to say: I get a sense of personal satisfaction from working well as part of a team [YL16].

[YL17]I enjoyed reading your essay. It is rather short, perhaps you could have expanded it but writing about how members of your community see you as well?

[YL18]I still feel that your essay is rather short (as I indicated last month) and that you could expand and therefore develop your English further.
